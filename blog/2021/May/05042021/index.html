Worthy Update.
4 May 2021

Hey person hey,

You may have noticed that there was a much longer break between my last post on April 11 and today. I did that for a few reasons. The first reason was that nothing of note had happened but I knew that some interesting things were coming up so I wanted to wait for them. Now that those interesting things have happened, I'm so excited to tell you about them.

Firstly, what we're all here for, I did some programming. WOOOO! I tried to continue the book but it was just soooo boring. If I didn't know anything about Python, it would have been super interesting but since I have some knowledge under my belt, it was boring. So I did what I needed to do, I skipped all the way to the projects sections. I'm working on my first project now, an alien invasion video game. Essentially, I'm building a game where aliens invade and it's up to a little space ship to keep the planet safe. Does it sound like an old school game you have already heard about? That's because it is. I'm enjoying it but I did get a little pre-occupied with some other stuff.

Exercise, wise? I'm on week three of Insanity Max 30 and honestly, they named it very well. It's absolutely crazy but it only takes 30 minutes. It's tiring but I do feel significantly better about my cardio already and I still have more than a month of the program to go.

I finished my Italian A2 course and B1 starts on Monday. Japanese is awkwardly paused.

But the real reason for the wait was that I got a job! In the past three weeks, I've gotten three job offers. The Business Analyst role, I turned down. Then it was down to two that I was interested in. I ended up not taking the Junior Data Analyst role which is absolutely nuts, right? I'm not 100% sure that I made the right decision but I made a decision and now I need to stick with it. After four months of being unemployed, I am once again working.

Having a job is hard when you've been out of the game so I'm allowing myself time to relax. I hope that it doesn't get in the way of all of the other things that I'm doing so I will try to make a plan and stick with it. I hope you enjoyed the update and hopefully next time I'll be done with the game and maybe you can see it.

Till next time,
Cope.
